Overview

Purpose

The purpose of this website is to give me an opportunity to learn and grow as a student, and a programmer. This is a place where I can practice what I have learned and apply it directly. Over time I hope to keep expanding both my knowledge and this webpage and be able to display all that I had learned through this course.

Audience

This webpage is for anyone who wants to see me progress and learn, as well as to check on my progress. I also plan on using this sight to show off some of the things I have created, as well as show of some cool creations made by others. So if you enjoy Legos and models, this might just be the website for you!
